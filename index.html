Chicken Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 2 Glasses of Rice
  • 1 KG Chicken
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 Lemons
  • 1 Spoon Jira seeds
  • 2 Biryani leaves
  • 8 cloves
  • All other spices and masalas
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Curd
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes

Steps:

  1. Take a pan and pour some oil in it
  2. Add spices and masala items and Biryani leaves and onions and tomatoes and Ginger garlic in the pan.
  3. Take a bowl chicken and add Lemon water and curd and pour some oil in it and add some spices and mix it well.
  4. Add this bowl chicken into the pan with previous ingredients
  5. On separate stove preaper a semi cooked rice
  6. Now take that semi cooked rice add it into the pan containing the chicken and many other ingredients.
  7. Wait some time and now your biryani ready
